About me

Program Lead, Tech Evangelist, Data Science Enthusiast, Diversity and Inclusion Advocate

Diversity, Equity and Inclusion


Researched and built strategies for diversity, equity and inclusion for women in technology in India. Developed my network with various women in tech communities, D&I champions and organisations supporting women in tech in India. As Verna Meyers says, "Diversity is being invited to the party. Inclusion is being asked to dance."

Data Science and Machine Learning

Received the 'Women in AI Leadership 2020' award by Analytics India Magazine and completed the 'GE Analytics Engineer Certification' in 2016. Interested in hackathon participation and various certifications in topics of interest. As W. Edwards Deming says, "Without data, you're just another person with an opinion."

Leadership



Recognised with the 'Lead Like A Girl 2020' fellowship. Over the last three years, I have been managing projects, programs and people. My strength lies in adding some method to the madness and practicing empathetic leadership. As Peter Drucker says, "Management is doing things right; Leadership is doing the right things."

Community Building


Involved with various tech communities in India for over a decade now with about five years of experience in building, nurturing and scaling communities. I have received a lot from communities and am striving to find ways to pay it forward. As Helen Keller says, "Alone, we can do so little; together, we can do so much!".
