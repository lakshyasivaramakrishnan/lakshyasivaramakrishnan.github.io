About me

I realise my four pillars of interest through these roles: Technical Program Manager, Tech Evangelist, Founder of Prayogshaala, Diversity and Inclusion Advocate

Diversity, Equity and Inclusion


Researched and built strategies for diversity, equity and inclusion for women in technology in India. Developed my network with various women in tech communities, D&I champions and organisations supporting women in tech in India. As Verna Meyers says, "Diversity is being invited to the party. Inclusion is being asked to dance."

Data Science and Machine Learning

Received the 'Women in AI Leadership' Award by Analytics India Magazine in 2020 and completed the 'GE Analytics Engineer Certification' in 2016. Interested in hackathon participation and various certifications in topics of interest. As W. Edwards Deming says, "Without data, you're just another person with an opinion."

Leadership



Recognised with the 'Lead Like A Girl 2020' fellowship. I have been managing projects, programs and people. My strength lies in adding some method to the madness and practicing empathetic leadership. A certified #IAmRemarkable facilitator. As Peter Drucker says, "Management is doing things right; Leadership is doing the right things."

Community Building


Involved with various tech communities in India for over a decade now with about five years of experience in building, nurturing and scaling communities. I have received a lot from communities and am striving to find ways to pay it forward. As Helen Keller says, "Alone, we can do so little; together, we can do so much!".

Prayogshaala

Weekend online bootcamp to nurture young women in technology

REGISTER NOW



Prayogshaala aims to provide tech and applied skills education for engineering college girls in India through a weekend online bootcamp to help them thrive in the technology industry.

Selected participants will have an opportunity to enhance their skills on leadership, communication, personal branding and technology.

For more queries, feel free to mail us at writetoprayogshaala@gmail.com.

Lakshya Sivaramakrishnan is a Technical Program Manager at Google with the Payments Merchant team. She has managed the Women Techmakers Program in India for about five years, prior to this role. She has founded the Prayogshaala program which is a one week online bootcamp to nurture young women in technology. She has received the "Women in AI Leadership Award" by Analytics India Magazine and the "Women in Corporate Allies - Emerging Diversity Champion Award" by Women's Web in 2020. She is a TEDx speaker and a Shenomics "Lead Like a Girl Fellow". She enjoys nurturing communities and empowering women to join, thrive, and lead in the tech industry.

Prior to Google, she has been a Consultant with Cerner Healthcare and Software Engineer at GE Healthcare. She also holds a GE Analytics India Certification and is an #IAmRemarkable facilitator. She has delivered 225+ talks and workshops on technical topics and applied skills as a part of communities, institutes and organisations. She has completed her Masters in Information Technology from International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore and Bachelors in Computer Science from Sir M Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology.
